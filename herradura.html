Herradura

Herradura, literally "horseshoe" is a hill to the south of Barranco that offers a great view of the city. It is located in the Chorrillos neighborhood. At the base of the hill there is also a popular beach.

Several monuments can be seen from the top of La Herradura. Some of them are shown here.
